Scoring Designs for Sourdough Bread

Instructions

Step 1 - Gather Materials

  • Unbaked sourdough round
  • Scoring blade
  • Flour
  • Optional: spray bottle full of water



Step 2 - Score the bread with a design of your choice

Hold the blade perpendicular to the dough. There's no need to press hard or score deeply, but your cut does need to be deep enough that the surface of the dough doesn't fuse back together when it is baking. I usually aim for 1/2" deep.

Try out different designs!




Step 3 - Bake your bread and enjoy!

If you wish, spray your bread with water before baking. Then, bake according to the recipe's instructions. Enjoy!
