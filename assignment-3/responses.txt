
What is the relationship between an IP address and the DNS?
A DNS (Domain Name Server) serves as the translation system for IP addresses. This allows the end user to enter a URL like www.amazon.com instead of remeber a stream of numbers.  

What is a cookie?
Cookies are small blocks of data generated by web servers and place on user devices through the web browser.

What are the three essential parts of a URL?
The protocol, the domain name, and the path are the three parts of a URL.
